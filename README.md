# 📊 Vrinda Store Annual Sales Dashboard (Excel)

## 🔍 Overview
This project presents an interactive Excel dashboard analyzing Vrinda Store’s 2022 sales data.  
The objective is to extract actionable insights on customer behavior, sales trends, and operational performance to support business growth in 2023–2024.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Identify key customer segments driving revenue  
- Analyze monthly sales and order trends  
- Evaluate channel and regional performance  
- Measure order fulfillment efficiency

## 📌 Key KPIs & Visualizations

### 📈 Orders vs Sales (Monthly Trend)
- Compares total sales and order volume across months  
- Helps identify peak and low-performing periods  

---

### 👩‍🦰 Gender-wise Sales Contribution
- Women: 64%  
- Men: 36%  
- Indicates women are the dominant buyers  

---

### 📦 Order Status Distribution
- Delivered: 92%  
- Cancelled: 3%  
- Returned: 3%  
- Refunded: 2%  

---

### 🌍 Top 5 States by Sales
- Maharashtra: 2.99M  
- Karnataka: 2.64M  
- Uttar Pradesh: 2.10M  
- Telangana: 1.71M  
- Tamil Nadu: 1.67M  

---

### 👥 Age & Gender (Order Distribution)
- Adult Women: 34.6%  
- Adult Men: 15.5%  
- Teenagers and seniors contribute less  

---

### 🛒 Channel-wise Orders
- Amazon: 35.5%  
- Myntra: 23.4%  
- Flipkart: 21.6%  
- Others (Ajio, Meesho, Nalli): Remaining share  

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- Women contribute ~64% of total sales  
- Age group 30–49 is the highest contributing segment  
- Top 3 states generate the majority of revenue  
- Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra dominate sales channels  
- 92% of orders are successfully delivered  

---

## 🧠 Strategic Insights
- Revenue is concentrated in a specific customer segment (women, 30–49)  
- Heavy dependence on marketplace platforms  
- Geographic concentration in a few states
